Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a trip?

You can register for a trip by navigating to the "Book Trip" section in the menu. Select your route, preferred time, and confirm your booking.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 2 hours before the trip departure time through the "My Trips" tab.

What are the return trip options?

Return trips run hourly starting from 1:00 PM until 6:00 PM. You must book your return slot separately.

What happens if I miss my bus?

If you miss your bus, your registration will be marked as no-show. You will need to wait for the next available bus and request a walk-in seat based on availability.

How do I change my pickup point?

You can update your default pickup point from your Account Settings, or change it for a specific trip during the booking process.
